My Background

I bought a computer, taught myself how to program and found I loved computers and programming! I went to college, got a degree in Computer Engineering and got a job in computer hardware engineering. I recently left my hardware engineering job and am excited to be getting back to programming.

Why Web Programming?

I paid my way through college programming production, scheduling, payroll, etc for a small manufacturing company. I found I loved programming and all the useful and creative things you can do with programming. The web has absolutely transformed what we can do and how we accomplish our goals. I'm very excited to get back to programming and be a part of this web innovation tidal wave!
