My Background

After high school I was working in a manufacturing plant and my boss asked me to research computers, buy one and program it. I bought a computer, taught myself how to program and found I loved computers and programming! I went to college and got a degree in Computer Engineering. I paid my way through college by writing programs to computerize the manufacturing plant operations (production, delivery scheduling, payroll, etc.) After graduating I got a Computer Hardware Engineering job at Intel. I recently left my hardware engineering job and went to Coder Camps to learn web development (.NET/C#, JavaScript, SQL, HTML, CSS, etc.) I also learned Xamarin and built my first mobile phone app. I am VERY excited to be back in software development!
